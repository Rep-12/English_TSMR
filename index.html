What is Florida Universitaria?
Welcome to Florida Universitaria!
Florida Universitaria is a highschool/college located on Avenida Rey Don Jaime I, in Catarroja.

Florida Universitaria offers a spacious centre with 4 classroom buildings, library, a cafeteria, and an International Department.

Its library has a few laptops and a few rooms, facilitating the study environment.
Its cafeteria, which is very wide, has lots of products and also a network for students to study.
